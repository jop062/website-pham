About Me

A bit about my background and approach.

I'm a data-focused problem solver building at the intersection of AI, machine learning, and cloud systems. I study Data Analytics at UC San Diego, where I focus on turning complex data into reliable, production-ready solutions.

I design and deploy machine learning systems that move beyond experimentation into practical use. My work centers on building scalable pipelines, training models responsibly, and ensuring systems are measurable, efficient, and maintainable.

My mission is to translate data into systems that create real-world impact, not just theoretical results.

I believe strong engineering means clarity over complexity, systems over scripts, and measurable performance over hype.

Long term, I aim to build and lead intelligent systems that operate at scale—where machine learning, infrastructure, and responsible AI converge.

Outside of technology, I stay active through tennis, fishing, and strategy-driven games like Tetris. Disciplines that reinforce focus, iteration, and competitive thinking.
